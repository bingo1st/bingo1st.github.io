<b>ANZSCO 3341 水管工主要职责 Tasks Include - GKVisa<br />
</b><br />
• 研究设计书、图纸和说明书，以确定水管系统的布局和需要的材料。studying blueprints, drawings and specifications to determine the layout of plumbing systems and materials required <br />
• 设置和安装冷热水系统和相关设备。setting out and installing hot and cold water systems and associated equipment <br />
• 安装水性消防系统，包括消防栓、消防卷盘和自动喷水灭火系统。installing water-based fire protections systems, including fire hydrants, hose reels and sprinkler systems <br />
• 设计和安装卫生管道和供水系统、排放管道和卫生设备。designing and installing sanitary plumbing and water supply systems, discharge pipes and sanitary fixtures <br />
• 制造和安装排水立管和垃圾堆放区。fabricating and installing soil and waste stacks <br />
• 装配和安装机械服务设备、通风和调节设备以及小口径加热系统。assembling and installing mechanical services plant, air handling and conditioning equipment and small bore heating systems <br />
• 安装排污设备和处理系统。installing sewerage and effluent pumping equipment and disposal systems <br />
• 安装地下排水系统和相关的地面支持系统。installing below-ground drainage systems and associated ground support systems <br />
• 安装燃气用具、烟道和调压设备。installing gas appliances, flues and pressure regulating devices <br />
• 制造和安装金属屋面、防水物品和防水板。fabricating and installing metal roofing, rainwater goods and flashings <br />
